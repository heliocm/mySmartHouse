What is it?

About mySmartHouse

mySmartHouse is a project made by students of Federal University of Rio de Janeiro to improve the concept of a fully interactive house. It's Arduino based, so it has born to be cheap and easy to use. It is still in development, so feel free to join us on this project. Let's make the future cheaper and sooner!

Why should I use it?

Here are some motivations:

With mySmartHouse you can control anything in your house anytime and anywhere. You will be getting information about what's going on in your place like energy consumption, real-time statement of charges, temperature or even the weather outside. Besides that, you can turn on/off anything that is plugged on a power socket. Even doors or windows if your house is prepared to it! Just use it to do whatever you want!
